About Incidents

An incident is a formal record of a security event that requires coordinated response following the NIST incident response lifecycle.

Lifecycle Phases:
  • Detection & Analysis – Confirmed incident, initial investigation
  • Containment – Limiting impact and extent
  • Eradication – Removing the threat
  • Recovery – Restoring systems to normal
  • Post-Incident – Lessons learned, improvements
Best Practices:
  • Group related alerts for better context
  • Assign a clear incident commander
  • Document timeline and findings
  • Transition through phases as investigation progresses
