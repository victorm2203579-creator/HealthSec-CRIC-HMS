Email Configuration Guide
For Development (Console Backend)

Emails will be printed to the console. Set in .env:

EMAIL_BACKEND=django.core.mail.backends.console.EmailBackend
For Production (Gmail SMTP)

Use Gmail's app password. Set in .env:

EMAIL_BACKEND=django.core.mail.backends.smtp.EmailBackend
EMAIL_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
EMAIL_PORT=587
EMAIL_USE_TLS=True
EMAIL_HOST_USER=your-email@gmail.com
EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD=your-app-password
DEFAULT_FROM_EMAIL=HealthSec <noreply@healthsec.local>
Note: For Gmail, you must:
  1. Enable 2-factor authentication on your Google account
  2. Generate an App Password
  3. Use the app password in EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD (not your regular password)
